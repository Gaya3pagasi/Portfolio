Auréa

A brand identity for a fine jewelry house built entirely around gold — not just the traditional yellow, white, and rose, but a wider spectrum of blue, purple, and green gold for a modern generation of luxury.

Role

Brand & Visual Identity Designer

Year

2026

Tools

Brand Research, Moodboarding, AI Image Generation

Auréa takes its name from the Latin aurea, the feminine form of aureus — "golden." It's a fitting root for a brand built entirely around one material: gold, reimagined. The brief was to design an identity for a jewelry house that treats gold not as a single traditional colour but as a full spectrum — yellow, rose, and white sitting alongside blue, violet, and emerald gold — aimed at a modern generation who want heritage without the expected palette.

The concept, "Timeless Roots. Modern Blooms," runs through every part of the identity: a root system grounded in heritage that grows outward into new, unexpected colour. That idea shaped the moodboard — raw gold nuggets and molten gold texture on one side for heritage, a hand-drawn root-to-bloom motif to bridge old and new, and a saturated six-colour gold palette (yellow, rose, white, emerald, sapphire, and violet gold) to carry the "modern bloom" half of the story.

Because so much of the brand rests on a colour story that doesn't exist in real jewelry yet, reference photography alone couldn't show it. I used AI image generation, backed by research into real gold-plating and colour-treatment techniques, to visualise what tinted-gold rings and a floral, multi-gold necklace would actually look like worn — giving the identity a believable product world to sit inside before a single physical piece exists.

Typography pairs a serif display face (Playfair Display) for the wordmark with a clean sans (Montserrat) for supporting text — the same heritage/innovation contrast as the palette, translated into type. The result is a complete, presentation-ready brand board: name, concept, palette, materials, typography, and lifestyle imagery, all reinforcing one idea — heritage that's still allowed to evolve.
